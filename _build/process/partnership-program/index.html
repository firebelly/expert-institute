Our Services

Partnership benefits at a glance

Services included

01 Expert Search

Custom expert witness recruiting in any specialty or practice

Benefits

  • Expert candidates that meet all your criteria, no exceptions
  • Thorough professional and litigation history checks
  • Complimentary conference calls with candidates

02 Case Clinics

Save time and resources by screening potential cases for merit

Benefits

  • Review medical records with our in-house MDs
  • Identify strengths or weaknesses in the facts
  • Develop winning trial strategies

03 Challenge Studies

Research reports on the challenge history of any opposing expert

Benefits

  • View an expert’s full challenge history
  • Access summaries regarding challenge grounds and outcomes
  • Get in-depth explanations for exclusion or admission of testimony

04 Expert IQ™

Your personalized, online portal for managing expert witness casework

Benefits

  • Submit product requests and access your full case history all with one click
  • Schedule conference calls with experts through the IQ calendar
  • Receive real-time updates during every step of the process

05 Support Team

On-demand access to our entire team throughout every case

Benefits

  • Your designated customer success manager handles all case requests and troubleshooting
  • Your designated research manager spearheads your firm’s Expert Searches
  • Our entire medical team is at your disposal for Case Clinics in any practice area
