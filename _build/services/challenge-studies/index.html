Transform Your Expert Search and Diligence Process

Expert Witness Challenge Studies from The Expert Institute give you the tools to make confident choices with expert candidates and gain case-winning insights into opposing expertise - all while cutting costs and administrative overhead.

Gain total confidence in your experts

Select the best experts for your case based on challenge history data. Develop your trial strategy and limit exposure to admissibility challenges.

Extract intelligence on opposing expertise

Capitalize on gatekeeping challenges and prior testimony of opposing experts. Identify winning strategies and questions for deposition and cross examination.

Reduce costs and administrative waste

Streamline research and diligence processes without additional staff. Save billable hours for the most important aspects of your case.
